# Jobs Dashboard - Business Documentation

## Overview

The Jobs Dashboard provides real-time monitoring and analytics for queue jobs, failed jobs, and AI generation statistics in the MovieMind API. This dashboard enables operations teams to monitor system health, identify issues, and track performance metrics.

## Business Value

### 1. Operational Visibility
- **Real-time Monitoring**: Track queue status, job processing, and failures in real-time
- **Proactive Issue Detection**: Identify problems before they impact users
- **Performance Metrics**: Monitor job processing times and success rates

### 2. Cost Management
- **AI Usage Tracking**: Monitor AI generation jobs and their success rates
- **Resource Optimization**: Identify bottlenecks and optimize queue processing
- **Failure Analysis**: Understand failure patterns to reduce costs

### 3. Reliability
- **Failed Job Monitoring**: Quickly identify and address failed jobs
- **Queue Health**: Monitor queue depth and processing capacity
- **Trend Analysis**: Track failure rates and processing times over time

## Key Metrics

### Queue Statistics
- **Pending Jobs**: Jobs waiting to be processed
- **Processing Jobs**: Jobs currently being executed
- **Completed Jobs**: Successfully completed jobs
- **Failed Jobs**: Jobs that failed during execution

### Performance Metrics
- **Processing Times**: Average time to process jobs
- **Failure Rate**: Percentage of jobs that fail
- **Queue Depth**: Number of jobs in each queue
- **Throughput**: Jobs processed per hour/day

### AI Generation Metrics
- **Success Rate**: Percentage of successful AI generations
- **By Entity Type**: Statistics for movies, persons, TV series
- **By Status**: Pending, done, failed counts
